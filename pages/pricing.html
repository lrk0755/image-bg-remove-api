Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How does "Pay As You Go" work?
Buy credits once and use them whenever you want. Credits never expire. Perfect for occasional use or trying the service.
Q: What's included in Unlimited plans?
Unlimited plans give you unlimited background removal credits every month. No counting, no running out. Use as much as you need.
Q: Can I cancel anytime?
Yes! Monthly subscriptions can be cancelled anytime. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period.
Q: What's the free trial for Yearly?
Yearly plan comes with a 7-day free trial. If you're not satisfied, cancel within 7 days and you won't be charged.
Q: Do credits roll over?
Pay As You Go credits never expire. Monthly/Yearly subscriptions reset each period - unused credits don't roll over.
Q: What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay.
